# 4. Registering and Familiarizing with AI Design Platforms

## Choose Your Starting Point

Before starting, you need to choose a primary AI website building platform. This book recommends starting with **Durable** or **Wix ADI** because they best fit the core concept of "AI generation."

Let's use **Durable** as an example to walk you through registration and basic setup.

## Registering Durable

### Step 1: Go to the Official Website

In your browser, enter: `https://durable.ai`

### Step 2: Click Get Started

Click "Get Started" or "Start Building" on the homepage.

### Step 3: Choose Registration Method

You can choose:
- **Google account login**: Fastest and most convenient
- **Email registration**: Need to set a password

Using Google account login is recommended to avoid remembering a password.

### Step 4: Fill in Basic Information

After registration, you need to fill in:
- **Your name**: For addressing you
- **Business type**: Restaurant, freelancer, e-commerce, etc. (can choose "Other")
- **Location**: For localization settings

### Step 5: Complete Verification

Some tools require email verification, check your email and click the verification link.

## Building Your First Website

### Click "AI Builder"

Click "AI Builder" in the left menu, which is the core function for generating websites.

### Enter Your Website Description

Durable will ask you: "What would you like to build?"

This is a crucial step. You can enter:

**Simple Example**:
```
Personal photographer portfolio website
```

**Detailed Example**:
```
I want a minimalist style personal photographer portfolio website,
with black and white as the main color scheme,
including home page, portfolio page, about me page, and contact page
```

**English Example** (Durable mainly has English interface):
```
A minimalist photography portfolio website with black and white theme,
including home page, portfolio gallery, about me, and contact page
```

### Wait for AI Generation

After clicking generate, AI will start working. This process usually takes:
- **Fast mode**: About 30 seconds to 1 minute
- **Complete mode**: About 2-3 minutes

### View Generation Results

After generation completes, you'll see a preview. This is the website AI automatically built for you!

## Wix ADI Registration Process

If you choose Wix ADI, the process is similar:

### Step 1: Go to Wix ADI

URL: `https://www.wix.com/adi`

### Step 2: Click "Get Started"

### Step 3: Answer AI's Questions

Wix ADI will ask you a series of questions to understand your needs:

1. **What type of website do you want to build?**
   - Personal website
   - Business website
   - Online store
   - Other

2. **What is the main purpose of the website?**
   - Showcase work
   - Sell products
   - Provide services
   - Share content

3. **Do you have a brand name?**

4. **What features do you want?**
   - Blog
   - Online booking
   - Membership system
   - Contact form

### Step 4: AI Generation and Adjustment

After answering questions, Wix ADI will generate a website that you can further adjust on this basis.

## Understanding Basic Website Structure

Before continuing, let's understand the basic components of a website:

### Common Pages

| Page Name | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Home | Entry point of the website, introducing overall content |
| About | Introduce personal or company background |
| Portfolio/Services | Showcase your work or services |

| Contact | Provide contact methods or forms |

## Familiarizing with Editor Interface

Most AI building platforms have similar editor functions:

### Drag and Drop

Find the element you want to move, hold the mouse and drag to the new position. This is the basic function of modern website editors.

### Click to Edit

Most text can be directly clicked to modify. Double-click on text blocks to enter edit mode.

### Right-click Menu

Right-click on an element to see options:
- **Copy**
- **Delete**
- **Edit settings**
- **Move to another section**

### Settings Panel

Usually after clicking an element, a settings panel appears on the right or below, where you can adjust:
- Colors
- Fonts
- Size
- Spacing
- Animation effects
